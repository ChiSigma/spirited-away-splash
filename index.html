FAQ

What's different from last year?

Last year was our first time putting on this event, and we learned a lot while managing 275+ ticket sales.
1) We have a promotion that will allow you to get a free or discounted ticket!
2) We're working closely with the Spirit of Boston team to make sure boarding the boat is an easier process.
3) We pray everyday to our different gods and deities that the weather will be better *knock on wood*.
n) If you have any suggestions that you think we missed, please let us know! We're open to anything that will make this evening a better one for you and all of our guests.

Are you still selling tickets?

Yes, and we will continue to do so until we sell out or set sail - whichever comes first.

Do I have to be 21+ to attend?

Nope! We've just been keeping track of people's ages so that we can give the Spirit of Boston a heads up on how much booze to stock the paid bars with.

Are you guys making a profit from this?

When we came up with the idea to organize this event, our goal was to make it a good time for everyone. Since cheaper is almost always better, we decided to sell the tickets at cost. We'd rather have a fun time than make a profit

What's the deal with the open bar event?

We were approached by another organization, and they were interested in partnering with us for an event on March 15th. Our arrangement is that we will bring the people, and they will provide the beverages. Location and additional details will be revealed at a later date.

What if I can go to the open bar but not the boat party?

If that's the case, refer someone to the boat party and you will both be eligible to attend the open bar. There is a space on the sign-up form for someone to include the email of the person that referred them. As soon as they pay for their cruise ticket, you (the referrer) will be eligible to attend the open bar as well.

Is this for Northeastern students only?

We are planning this event to try to get as many groups of people together as possible. Everyone is welcome. Last year, we had students attending from 7 states and 26 different schools.

Is this actually happening?

Yep. We signed a contract, so this boat will be setting sail with or without you - we hope you choose the prior.

Do I have to pay for my ticket right away?

Of course not. We're all college kids and get that you might not be able to pay right away. Feel free to sign-up and pay when you're good and ready.

What if I don't know what I'm doing that day?

That's okay! Go ahead and sign-up anyway. There are no strings attached to filling out the form. That way, we can get an idea of how many people are interested, and it makes planning on our end easier.

Is this the same boat as some of the other aquatic events?

Nope - at least not the larger events. The Spirit of Boston is newly renovated and designed specifically for this kind of event. We have a few links on this site and the Facebook page with info on the venue if you want to know more.

After I've paid, can I return my ticket?

Unfortunately, we have to have a minimum number of people sign up for the event, so we cannot accept refunds. If for some reason this event has to be cancelled, you will be reimbursed for your ticket. If you resell your ticket, please notify us at info@chisigma.co so we can update the new ticket information on our end.
